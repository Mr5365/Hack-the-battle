About Us

We are a team of passionate developers who love creating simple, classic games for everyone to enjoy. Our mission is to bring engaging and fun experiences to your browser, focusing on the core joy of gaming without any of the complications.

Our Journey

This journey began with a single animated image and a simple idea: to build a library of games using fundamental web technologies. From there, we've brought a variety of games to life, including:

  • Arcade Classics: Our collection features high-flying Flappy Bird, fast-paced Ping-Pong, and the timeless challenge of T-Rex.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great games don't need complicated setups or powerful hardware. That's why we build all our projects using the foundational trio of the web: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This approach ensures our games are lightweight, accessible, and instantly playable on any device.

Thank you for playing our games. We hope you have as much fun with them as we had creating them.
